This blog is meant to be light.

Not a publishing empire, not a second WordPress, and not a place where every passing thought has to turn into a formal essay. Mostly it gives me a clean home for process notes, companion posts for videos, and the occasional how-to when something is useful enough to write down.

Sometimes a collage or a song wants a little context. Sometimes a video deserves a list of links, tools, references, or the story of how it came together. Sometimes I may just want a quiet note here instead of sending it out into whatever the algorithm is doing that week.

That is the whole idea.

The blog can stay sparse and still be useful.
